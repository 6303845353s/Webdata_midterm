Albert Einstein

About Me

I’m Albert Einstein, theoretical physicist extraordinaire. My theory of relativity (E = mc²) transformed modern physics. I have been awarded the Nobel Prize in Physics for my groundbreaking work, and I continue to inspire curiosity about the universe. I was never one to follow convention. My theoretical contributions—most notably, the theory of relativity—altered the course of scientific history.
